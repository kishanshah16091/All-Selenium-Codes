How to handel Calendars:
1) click on the Calendar
2) locate the month and get the text
3) If the desire text is not visible  - --> we will keep clicking on next and keep updating the text of the month
4) as soon as we found our desired month --- > grab all the cell from table
5) Loop through the cells
6) once the cell value is matched with our condition ---> click on the match date
